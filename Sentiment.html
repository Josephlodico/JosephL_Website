Sentiment

Description

Sentiment is a psychological horror game that delves with themes of a dysfunctional family. You play as a little boy trying to take control of a situation that is beyond him.

Game Jam Info

  • Role: 3D Artist, UI Programmer
  • Team size: 8
  • Awards: "Best Audio" and "Participant's Pick"
  • Engine: Unreal Engine 5
  • Tools: Perforce, Autodesk Maya
  • Duration: 48 hours
  • Date: April 17 - April 19
  • Theme: You Are Not Alone
