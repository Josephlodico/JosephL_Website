ShopSimulator Video

This is how our Shop Simulator Works, First Your Enter a name then,You will see 4 prompts 1 makes you Enter the Shop, 2 Makes you Enter The Minigame, and 3 makes you see your purchase history, then you press 0 if you want to quit the program.

The Shop (PROMPT 1)

In the Shop there are multiple Items You could purchase items, But in order to purchase you have to earn money by playing minigames.

The Minigame (PROMPT 2)

In the Minigame there is only one minigame you could play to earn your money (currency) and its Rock, Paper, Scizzors. And the person you play against is an AI Called Lana, She is programmed to randomly pick a choice so it does not make it easy.

Purchase History (PROMPT 3)

the Purchase History is the place to see all the items you purchased while in the program.
