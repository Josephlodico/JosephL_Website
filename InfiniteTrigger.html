About InfiniteTrigger

Infinite Trigger is a Zombie Shooting Game i Worked on in A group of 3 people for My Game Engine 2 Class. The group Members are: Anthony Vitalie D'ermes, Lorenzo Marquez and me. The Style we were aiming for was kind of similar to Cod Zombies Were you had to survive Waves by killing zombies.

InfiniteTrigger/GameSplash

For our GameSplash design, We where trying to fit the vibe of a Zombie type color for our Logo. With the Text being a little Scary looking with a Gun Being Huge in the logo since its our Main mechanic in the Game.

InfiniteTrigger/(Gameplay, Shop System)

For the gameplay in Infinite Trigger You start off By Spawning in with a pistol with an amount of bullets you could shoot with. Then the player has to survive the Wave by Killing the Zombies coming after them, if your HP reaches 0 the player dies then you have to restart.
